Impact Driven

The average price based algorithm , namely TWAP and VWAP, represent the first generation of impact-driven algorithms.

These algorithms aim to reduce the effect that buynig/ or selling an stock has on the traded securitys price.

Remember : Buying a large quantity of one stock signals an increase in the demand for the stock. An increase in the demand means that sellers can raise the price.

And Vice Versa !

These Algorithms aim to hide the effect of buying or selling large quantities of stock has on the assests price by hiding the purchase over a certain time frame.
